Summary: The goal of this lab is to prepare fixatives for the anterior capsule and limbs, induce a cataract in a pig eye, and set up the wet lab space for the experiment.

Procedure:
- Set up shelves for storage of instruments and supplies.
- Locate the sink area for utensil cleaning.
- Prepare a fixative for the anterior capsule to reduce elasticity.
- Prepare fixatives for injection into the limbs to induce a cataract.
- Use the microwave to induce a cataract in a pig eye.
- Store the pig eye in the mini fridge.
- Set up the operating microscope with the teaching scope for instruction.
- Lay out all the instruments on the work table.
- Pin the pig eye down with pens at the location of the rectus muscles.
