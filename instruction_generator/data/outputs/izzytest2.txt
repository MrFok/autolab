

Summary: This lab focuses on preparing a wet lab space for cataract surgery. 

Procedure: 
- Set up shelves for storage of instruments and supplies. 
- Set up an area with a sink for cleaning utensils. 
- Prepare fixatives for the anterior capsule and limbs. 
- Use a microwave to induce a cataract. 
- Store the big eye in a mini fridge. 
- Set up an operating microscope with a teaching scope. 
- Lay out all instruments. 
- Pin the eye down with pens located where the rectus muscles would be.
